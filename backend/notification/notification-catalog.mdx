This documentation provides a comprehensive catalog of all notification types in the PropWise CRM system, including their triggers, recipients, behaviors, and available actions.

<Note>
This catalog is the source of truth based on `notification-type.enum.ts`, `notification-type.registry.ts`, and domain listeners under `src/modules/notification/listeners/`.
</Note>

## System Conventions

<Info>
**Sender Convention**: The "sender" is the acting user. In most bulk notification paths, the sender is filtered OUT of the recipient list unless explicitly opted into sender delivery.

**Channels**: Registry candidates are evaluated based on user preferences and per-reminder overrides to determine final delivery channels.

**Setting Visibility**: Some notification types require specific permissions to appear in user preference settings. This is cosmetic only - delivery logic remains unchanged.
</Info>

### Categories and Actions

**Categories**: `properties` (formerly `real_estate`) · `crm` · `access` (formerly `team`) · `system` · `messaging`

**Available Actions**:
- `navigate` - Opens the linked entity (available in both app and email)
- `approve`/`reject`/`rsvp_*` - In-app-only actions that change notification status
- Email notifications include deep-link buttons for `navigate` actions only

<Warning>
Interactive actions (approve/reject/RSVP) require recipients to open the app and are not available via email.
</Warning>

## Properties Notifications

### Unit Access

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Unit Access Request">
    **Category**: `properties` · **Subcategory**: `unit_access`  
    **Priority**: HIGH

    **Trigger**: An agent requests access to a unit's details  
    **Sender**: Requesting agent  
    **Recipients**: Unit manager  
    **Purpose**: Manager must approve/reject access  
    **Actions**: View entity · Approve · Reject (with reason) · View Unit · View Deal
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Unit Access Approved">
    **Category**: `properties` · **Subcategory**: `unit_access`  
    **Priority**: HIGH

    **Trigger**: Manager approves a unit access request  
    **Sender**: Approver  
    **Recipients**: Original requester  
    **Purpose**: Tell requester they now have access  
    **Actions**: View Unit
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Unit Access Rejected">
    **Category**: `properties` · **Subcategory**: `unit_access`  
    **Priority**: MEDIUM

    **Trigger**: Manager rejects a unit access request  
    **Sender**: Rejecter  
    **Recipients**: Original requester  
    **Purpose**: Tell requester the request was denied (+reason)  
    **Actions**: None
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
Unit access notifications require any real-estate or sales permission for setting visibility.
</Note>

### Listing Approval Workflow

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Listing Approval Requested">
    **Category**: `properties` · **Subcategory**: `listing`  
    **Priority**: HIGH

    **Trigger**: A non-publisher submits a listing for approval  
    **Sender**: Submitter  
    **Recipients**: Org `real_estate.manage` approvers  
    **Purpose**: Approve or reject the submission  
    **Actions**: Review listing
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Listing Approved">
    **Category**: `properties` · **Subcategory**: `listing`  
    **Priority**: HIGH

    **Trigger**: A manager approves a submitted listing  
    **Sender**: Approver  
    **Recipients**: Listing publisher (`createdBy`)  
    **Purpose**: Listing approved (may be live)  
    **Actions**: View listing
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Listing Rejected">
    **Category**: `properties` · **Subcategory**: `listing`  
    **Priority**: HIGH

    **Trigger**: A manager rejects a submitted listing  
    **Sender**: Rejecter  
    **Recipients**: Listing publisher (`createdBy`)  
    **Purpose**: Fix and resubmit (+reason)  
    **Actions**: View listing
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Listing Deleted">
    **Category**: `properties` · **Subcategory**: `listing`  
    **Priority**: MEDIUM

    **Trigger**: A listing is deleted by someone other than its publisher  
    **Sender**: Deleter  
    **Recipients**: Listing publisher (`createdBy`)  
    **Purpose**: Their listing was removed  
    **Actions**: None
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
Listing notifications require `real_estate.view`, `real_estate.manage`, or `real_estate.listing.publish` permissions for setting visibility.
</Note>

## CRM Notifications

### Lead Assignment & Stakeholder

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Lead Assigned">
    **Category**: `crm` · **Subcategory**: `leads`  
    **Priority**: MEDIUM

    **Trigger**: A lead is assigned to a user/team  
    **Sender**: Assigner  
    **Recipients**: New stakeholders (excluding assigner)  
    **Purpose**: Notify assignees of new lead  
    **Actions**: View Lead
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Lead Unassigned">
    **Category**: `crm` · **Subcategory**: `leads`  
    **Priority**: MEDIUM

    **Trigger**: A lead is returned to the pool  
    **Sender**: Performer  
    **Recipients**: Former stakeholders (excluding performer)  
    **Purpose**: They lost the lead  
    **Actions**: None
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Lead Claimed">
    **Category**: `crm` · **Subcategory**: `leads`  
    **Priority**: MEDIUM

    **Trigger**: A user claims a lead from the pool  
    **Sender**: Claiming user  
    **Recipients**: Other stakeholders  
    **Purpose**: A pool lead was taken  
    **Actions**: View Lead
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Lead Stakeholder Added">
    **Category**: `crm` · **Subcategory**: `leads`  
    **Priority**: MEDIUM

    **Trigger**: A user/team added as lead stakeholder (non-system-initiated)  
    **Sender**: Adder  
    **Recipients**: Added user  
    **Purpose**: Informed of assignment + commission  
    **Actions**: View Lead
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Lead Stakeholder Removed">
    **Category**: `crm` · **Subcategory**: `leads`  
    **Priority**: MEDIUM

    **Trigger**: A stakeholder removed from a lead  
    **Sender**: Remover  
    **Recipients**: Removed user (not self)  
    **Purpose**: Informed of removal  
    **Actions**: None
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Lead Stakeholder Commission Changed">
    **Category**: `crm` · **Subcategory**: `leads`  
    **Priority**: LOW

    **Trigger**: A lead stakeholder's commission % changes  
    **Sender**: Changer  
    **Recipients**: Affected user  
    **Purpose**: Commission updated  
    **Actions**: View Lead
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Lead Primary Assignee Assigned">
    **Category**: `crm` · **Subcategory**: `leads`  
    **Priority**: MEDIUM

    **Trigger**: A user or team-pool row is made primary on a lead  
    **Sender**: Changer  
    **Recipients**: Affected user, or each team leader when the new primary is a team pool (not self)  
    **Purpose**: Now primary owner  
    **Actions**: View Lead
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Lead Primary Assignee Unassigned">
    **Category**: `crm` · **Subcategory**: `leads`  
    **Priority**: MEDIUM

    **Trigger**: A user or team-pool row loses primary on a lead  
    **Sender**: Changer  
    **Recipients**: Affected user, or each team leader when the former primary was a team pool (not self)  
    **Purpose**: No longer primary  
    **Actions**: View Lead
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Lead Primary Assignee Row Changed">
    **Category**: `crm` · **Subcategory**: `leads`  
    **Priority**: MEDIUM

    **Trigger**: Primary assignment moves between stakeholder rows (same user)  
    **Sender**: Changer  
    **Recipients**: Affected user  
    **Purpose**: Their primary slot moved  
    **Actions**: View Lead
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
System-initiated stakeholder creations (distribution/escalation engines) emit `lead_assigned` instead of `lead_stakeholder_added` to avoid duplicate/confusing notifications.
</Tip>

### Activity Stakeholder Inbox

**Category**: `crm` · **Subcategory**: `activities`  
**Priority**: MEDIUM (all types)  
**Channels**: IN_APP + EMAIL candidates

Triggered when an activity is logged/scheduled on a lead or contact.

**Sender**: The performer  
**Recipients**: Lead + linked-contact stakeholders with `team.crm.view`/`team.crm.manage` (excluding performer)  
**Actions**: View record (navigates using the payload `entityType`/`entityId`)

<Check>
Activity notification types include:
- `activity_call_logged`
- `activity_email_logged` 
- `activity_sms_logged`
- `activity_meeting_scheduled`
- `activity_site_visit_scheduled`
- `activity_note_added`
- `activity_marked_important`
</Check>

<Warning>
Only create/schedule actions trigger notifications - editing an activity does NOT send notifications.
</Warning>
