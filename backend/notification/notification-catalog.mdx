<Info>
This catalog serves as the source of truth for all notification types in the PropWise CRM system. It's derived from `notification-type.enum.ts`, `notification-type.registry.ts`, and domain listeners under `src/modules/notification/listeners/`.
</Info>

## Overview

The notification system operates on several key principles:

<Note>
**Sender Convention**: The "sender" is the acting user. In most bulk notification paths, the sender is filtered OUT of the recipient list unless the caller explicitly opts into sender delivery.
</Note>

### Categories and Structure

Notifications are organized into five main categories:
- **Properties** (formerly `real_estate`) - Unit access and listing workflows
- **CRM** - Lead and contact management 
- **Access** (formerly `team`) - Team and permission management
- **System** - Platform-wide notifications
- **Messaging** - Communication notifications

### Channel Delivery

Channels are registry candidates, with final delivery determined by:
- User preferences settings
- Per-reminder overrides
- Permission-based visibility rules

### Available Actions

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Navigation Actions">
    - `navigate` - Opens the linked entity (available in both app and email)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Interactive Actions">
    - `approve`/`reject` - In-app-only approval actions
    - `rsvp_*` - RSVP responses for events
    
    <Warning>
    Interactive actions require recipients to open the app - email notifications only show deep-link buttons for `navigate` actions.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Properties — Unit Access

**Category:** `properties` · **Subcategory:** `unit_access`

<Info>
**Setting Visibility:** Requires any real-estate or sales permission. Users without these permissions won't see these notification toggles in their preferences UI.
</Info>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Unit Access Request">
    **Type:** `unit_access_request`
    
    **Trigger:** An agent requests access to a unit's details
    
    **Flow:**
    - **Sender:** Requesting agent
    - **Recipients:** Unit manager
    - **Priority:** HIGH
    - **Why:** Manager must approve/reject access request
    
    **Available Actions:**
    - View entity
    - Approve request
    - Reject (with reason)
    - View Unit
    - View Deal
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Unit Access Approved">
    **Type:** `unit_access_approved`
    
    **Trigger:** Manager approves a unit access request
    
    **Flow:**
    - **Sender:** Approver
    - **Recipients:** Original requester
    - **Priority:** HIGH
    - **Why:** Notify requester they now have access
    
    **Available Actions:**
    - View Unit
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Unit Access Rejected">
    **Type:** `unit_access_rejected`
    
    **Trigger:** Manager rejects a unit access request
    
    **Flow:**
    - **Sender:** Rejecter
    - **Recipients:** Original requester
    - **Priority:** MEDIUM
    - **Why:** Notify requester of denial with reason
    
    **Available Actions:** None
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Properties — Listing Approval Workflow

**Category:** `properties` · **Subcategory:** `listing`

<Info>
**Setting Visibility:** Requires `real_estate.view`, `real_estate.manage`, or `real_estate.listing.publish` permissions.
</Info>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Listing Approval Requested">
    **Type:** `listing_approval_requested`
    
    **Trigger:** A non-publisher submits a listing for approval
    
    **Flow:**
    - **Sender:** Submitter
    - **Recipients:** Organization users with `real_estate.manage` permissions
    - **Priority:** HIGH
    - **Why:** Listing needs approval or rejection
    
    **Available Actions:**
    - Review listing
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Listing Approved">
    **Type:** `listing_approved`
    
    **Trigger:** A manager approves a submitted listing
    
    **Flow:**
    - **Sender:** Approver
    - **Recipients:** Listing publisher (`createdBy`)
    - **Priority:** HIGH
    - **Why:** Listing approved and may be live
    
    **Available Actions:**
    - View listing
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Listing Rejected">
    **Type:** `listing_rejected`
    
    **Trigger:** A manager rejects a submitted listing
    
    **Flow:**
    - **Sender:** Rejecter
    - **Recipients:** Listing publisher (`createdBy`)
    - **Priority:** HIGH
    - **Why:** Listing needs fixes and resubmission
    
    **Available Actions:**
    - View listing
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Listing Deleted">
    **Type:** `listing_deleted`
    
    **Trigger:** A listing is deleted by someone other than its publisher
    
    **Flow:**
    - **Sender:** Deleter
    - **Recipients:** Listing publisher (`createdBy`)
    - **Priority:** MEDIUM
    - **Why:** Notify publisher their listing was removed
    
    **Available Actions:** None
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## CRM — Lead Assignment & Stakeholder

**Category:** `crm` · **Subcategory:** `leads`

<Note>
System-initiated stakeholder creations (distribution/escalation engines) emit `lead_assigned` instead of `lead_stakeholder_added` to avoid duplicate notifications.
</Note>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Lead Assigned">
    **Type:** `lead_assigned`
    
    **Trigger:** A lead is assigned to a user/team
    
    **Flow:**
    - **Sender:** Assigner
    - **Recipients:** New stakeholders (excluding assigner)
    - **Priority:** MEDIUM
    - **Why:** Notify assignees of new lead
    
    **Available Actions:**
    - View Lead
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Lead Unassigned">
    **Type:** `lead_unassigned`
    
    **Trigger:** A lead is returned to the pool
    
    **Flow:**
    - **Sender:** Performer
    - **Recipients:** Former stakeholders (excluding performer)
    - **Priority:** MEDIUM
    - **Why:** They lost the lead
    
    **Available Actions:** None
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Lead Claimed">
    **Type:** `lead_claimed`
    
    **Trigger:** A user claims a lead from the pool
    
    **Flow:**
    - **Sender:** Claiming user
    - **Recipients:** Other stakeholders
    - **Priority:** MEDIUM
    - **Why:** A pool lead was taken
    
    **Available Actions:**
    - View Lead
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Stakeholder Added">
    **Type:** `lead_stakeholder_added`
    
    **Trigger:** A user/team added as lead stakeholder (non-system initiated)
    
    **Flow:**
    - **Sender:** Adder
    - **Recipients:** Added user
    - **Priority:** MEDIUM
    - **Why:** Informed of assignment and commission details
    
    **Available Actions:**
    - View Lead
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Stakeholder Removed">
    **Type:** `lead_stakeholder_removed`
    
    **Trigger:** A stakeholder is removed from a lead
    
    **Flow:**
    - **Sender:** Remover
    - **Recipients:** Removed user (not self)
    - **Priority:** MEDIUM
    - **Why:** Informed of removal
    
    **Available Actions:** None
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Commission Changed">
    **Type:** `lead_stakeholder_commission_changed`
    
    **Trigger:** A lead stakeholder's commission percentage changes
    
    **Flow:**
    - **Sender:** Changer
    - **Recipients:** Affected user
    - **Priority:** LOW
    - **Why:** Commission updated
    
    **Available Actions:**
    - View Lead
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Primary Assignee Assigned">
    **Type:** `lead_primary_assignee_assigned`
    
    **Trigger:** A user or team-pool row is made primary on a lead
    
    **Flow:**
    - **Sender:** Changer
    - **Recipients:** Affected user, or each team leader when the new primary is a team pool (not self)
    - **Priority:** MEDIUM
    - **Why:** Now primary owner
    
    **Available Actions:**
    - View Lead
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Primary Assignee Unassigned">
    **Type:** `lead_primary_assignee_unassigned`
    
    **Trigger:** A user or team-pool row loses primary status on a lead
    
    **Flow:**
    - **Sender:** Changer
    - **Recipients:** Affected user, or each team leader when the former primary was a team pool (not self)
    - **Priority:** MEDIUM
    - **Why:** No longer primary
    
    **Available Actions:**
    - View Lead
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Primary Assignee Row Changed">
    **Type:** `lead_primary_assignee_row_changed`
    
    **Trigger:** Primary assignment moves between stakeholder rows (same user)
    
    **Flow:**
    - **Sender:** Changer
    - **Recipients:** Affected user
    - **Priority:** MEDIUM
    - **Why:** Their primary slot moved
    
    **Available Actions:**
    - View Lead
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## CRM — Activity Stakeholder Inbox

**Category:** `crm` · **Subcategory:** `activities`

<Info>
These notifications are triggered when an activity is logged/scheduled on a lead or contact. All have MEDIUM priority and are candidates for both IN_APP and EMAIL delivery.
</Info>

**Common Flow:**
- **Sender:** Activity performer
- **Recipients:** Lead + linked-contact stakeholders with `team.crm.view`/`team.crm.manage` (excluding performer)
- **Priority:** MEDIUM
- **Available Actions:** View record (navigates using payload `entityType`/`entityId`)
