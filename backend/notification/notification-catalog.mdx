This catalog provides a comprehensive reference for all notification types in the PropWise CRM system. It serves as the source of truth for notification behavior, covering triggers, recipients, priorities, and available actions.

<Note>
This documentation is based on `notification-type.enum.ts`, `notification-type.registry.ts`, and domain listeners in `src/modules/notification/listeners/`, plus external emitters (AI agent, escalation, distribution cron, workflow engine, reminder scheduler).
</Note>

## Notification Conventions

<Info>
**Sender Definition**: The acting user. In most bulk notification paths, the sender is filtered OUT of the recipient list unless the caller explicitly opts into sender delivery.

**Actions**: Lists in-app notification actions available to recipients. `navigate` opens the linked entity, while `approve`/`reject`/`rsvp_*` are in-app-only actions that change notification status. Email notifications only send deep-link buttons for `navigate` actions.
</Info>

### Categories and Subcategories

- **properties** (formerly `real_estate`) - Property and listing related notifications
- **crm** - Customer relationship management notifications  
- **access** (formerly `team`) - Access control and permissions
- **system** - System-wide administrative notifications
- **messaging** - Communication and messaging notifications

<Warning>
Subcategories are derived from notification types and never stored in the database. See `NotificationSubcategory` enum for complete list.
</Warning>

### Setting Visibility

Some notification types include `visibilityPermissions` in the registry. When present, `NotificationPreferenceService.getPreferences()` filters these types from the preference catalog before sending to the frontend. Users without the required permissions don't see the toggle in the UI.

<Tip>
This filtering is cosmetic only - delivery logic remains unchanged and stakeholders still receive notifications regardless of UI visibility.
</Tip>

## Properties Notifications

### Unit Access

**Category**: `properties` • **Subcategory**: `unit_access`

<Note>
Setting visibility requires any real-estate or sales permission.
</Note>

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="unit_access_request">
**When sent**: An agent requests access to a unit's details  
**Sender**: Requesting agent  
**Recipients**: Unit manager  
**Why**: Manager must approve/reject access  
**Priority**: HIGH  
**Actions**: View entity • Approve • Reject (with reason) • View Unit • View Deal
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="unit_access_approved">
**When sent**: Manager approves a unit access request  
**Sender**: Approver  
**Recipients**: Original requester  
**Why**: Tell requester they now have access  
**Priority**: HIGH  
**Actions**: View Unit
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="unit_access_rejected">
**When sent**: Manager rejects a unit access request  
**Sender**: Rejecter  
**Recipients**: Original requester  
**Why**: Tell requester the request was denied (with reason)  
**Priority**: MEDIUM  
**Actions**: None
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Listing Approval Workflow

**Category**: `properties` • **Subcategory**: `listing`

<Note>
Setting visibility requires `real_estate.view`, `real_estate.manage`, or `real_estate.listing.publish` permission.
</Note>

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="listing_approval_requested">
**When sent**: A non-publisher submits a listing for approval  
**Sender**: Submitter  
**Recipients**: Org `real_estate.manage` approvers  
**Why**: Approve or reject the submission  
**Priority**: HIGH  
**Actions**: Review listing
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="listing_approved">
**When sent**: A manager approves a submitted listing  
**Sender**: Approver  
**Recipients**: Listing publisher (`createdBy`)  
**Why**: Listing approved (may be live)  
**Priority**: HIGH  
**Actions**: View listing
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="listing_rejected">
**When sent**: A manager rejects a submitted listing  
**Sender**: Rejecter  
**Recipients**: Listing publisher (`createdBy`)  
**Why**: Fix and resubmit (with reason)  
**Priority**: HIGH  
**Actions**: View listing
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="listing_deleted">
**When sent**: A listing is deleted by someone other than its publisher  
**Sender**: Deleter  
**Recipients**: Listing publisher (`createdBy`)  
**Why**: Their listing was removed  
**Priority**: MEDIUM  
**Actions**: None
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## CRM Notifications

### Lead Assignment & Stakeholder

**Category**: `crm` • **Subcategory**: `leads`

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="lead_assigned">
**When sent**: A lead is assigned to a user/team  
**Sender**: Assigner  
**Recipients**: New stakeholders (excluding assigner)  
**Why**: Notify assignees of new lead  
**Priority**: MEDIUM  
**Actions**: View Lead
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="lead_unassigned">
**When sent**: A lead is returned to the pool  
**Sender**: Performer  
**Recipients**: Former stakeholders (excluding performer)  
**Why**: They lost the lead  
**Priority**: MEDIUM  
**Actions**: None
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="lead_claimed">
**When sent**: A user claims a lead from the pool  
**Sender**: Claiming user  
**Recipients**: Other stakeholders  
**Why**: A pool lead was taken  
**Priority**: MEDIUM  
**Actions**: View Lead
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="lead_stakeholder_added">
**When sent**: A user/team added as lead stakeholder (non system-initiated)  
**Sender**: Adder  
**Recipients**: Added user  
**Why**: Informed of assignment and commission  
**Priority**: MEDIUM  
**Actions**: View Lead
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="lead_stakeholder_removed">
**When sent**: A stakeholder removed from a lead  
**Sender**: Remover  
**Recipients**: Removed user (not self)  
**Why**: Informed of removal  
**Priority**: MEDIUM  
**Actions**: None
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="lead_stakeholder_commission_changed">
**When sent**: A lead stakeholder's commission percentage changes  
**Sender**: Changer  
**Recipients**: Affected user  
**Why**: Commission updated  
**Priority**: LOW  
**Actions**: View Lead
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="lead_primary_assignee_assigned">
**When sent**: A user or team-pool row is made primary on a lead  
**Sender**: Changer  
**Recipients**: Affected user, or each team leader when the new primary is a team pool (not self)  
**Why**: Now primary owner  
**Priority**: MEDIUM  
**Actions**: View Lead
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="lead_primary_assignee_unassigned">
**When sent**: A user or team-pool row loses primary on a lead  
**Sender**: Changer  
**Recipients**: Affected user, or each team leader when the former primary was a team pool (not self)  
**Why**: No longer primary  
**Priority**: MEDIUM  
**Actions**: View Lead
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="lead_primary_assignee_row_changed">
**When sent**: Primary assignment moves between stakeholder rows (same user)  
**Sender**: Changer  
**Recipients**: Affected user  
**Why**: Their primary slot moved  
**Priority**: MEDIUM  
**Actions**: View Lead
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Info>
System-initiated stakeholder creations (distribution/escalation engines) emit `lead_assigned` instead of `lead_stakeholder_added` to avoid duplicate or confusing notifications.
</Info>

### Activity Stakeholder Inbox

**Category**: `crm` • **Subcategory**: `activities`

<Warning>
These notifications are triggered only when activities are created or scheduled. Editing an activity does NOT trigger notifications.
</Warning>

Triggered when an activity is logged/scheduled on a lead or contact:
- **Sender**: The performer
- **Recipients**: Lead + linked-contact stakeholders with `team.crm.view` or `team.crm.manage` (excluding performer)
- **Priority**: All MEDIUM
- **Channels**: IN_APP + EMAIL candidates

**Activity Types**:
- `activity_call_logged`
- `activity_email_logged`
- `activity_sms_logged`
- `activity_meeting_scheduled`
- `activity_site_visit_scheduled`
- `activity_note_added`
- `activity_marked_important`

**Actions (all types)**: View record (navigates using payload `entityType`/`entityId`)
