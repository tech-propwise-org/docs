This is the comprehensive catalog of all notification types in PropWise CRM, serving as the source of truth for notification triggers, recipients, and available actions.

<Note>
This catalog is derived from `notification-type.enum.ts`, `notification-type.registry.ts`, and the domain listeners under `src/modules/notification/listeners/` plus external emitters (AI agent, escalation, distribution cron, workflow engine, reminder scheduler).
</Note>

## Notification Conventions

<Info>
**Sender** = the acting user. In most bulk paths, the sender is filtered OUT of the recipient list unless the caller explicitly opts into sender delivery.

**Channels** are registry candidates; user preferences and per-reminder overrides decide the final delivery channels.
</Info>

### Actions Column
- `navigate` = opens the linked entity
- `approve`/`reject`/`rsvp_*` = in-app-only actions that change notification status
- Email sends entity deep-link buttons for `navigate` actions only
- Interactive actions (approve/reject/RSVP) require the recipient to open the app

### Categories
- `properties` (formerly `real_estate`)
- `crm`
- `access` (formerly `team`)
- `system`
- `messaging`

<Warning>
**Setting Visibility:** Some notification types carry a `visibilityPermissions` list in the registry. When present, `NotificationPreferenceService.getPreferences()` filters those types out of the preference catalog before sending it to the frontend. This is **cosmetic only** - delivery logic is unchanged.
</Warning>

## Properties Notifications

### Unit Access

**Category:** `properties` · **Subcategory:** `unit_access`

<Note>
Setting visibility requires any real-estate or sales permission.
</Note>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Unit Access Request">
    **Type:** `unit_access_request`
    
    **When sent:** An agent requests access to a unit's details
    
    **Sender:** Requesting agent
    
    **Recipients:** Unit manager
    
    **Why:** Manager must approve/reject access
    
    **Priority:** HIGH
    
    **Actions:** View entity · Approve · Reject (with reason) · View Unit · View Deal
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Unit Access Approved">
    **Type:** `unit_access_approved`
    
    **When sent:** Manager approves a unit access request
    
    **Sender:** Approver
    
    **Recipients:** Original requester
    
    **Why:** Tell requester they now have access
    
    **Priority:** HIGH
    
    **Actions:** View Unit
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Unit Access Rejected">
    **Type:** `unit_access_rejected`
    
    **When sent:** Manager rejects a unit access request
    
    **Sender:** Rejecter
    
    **Recipients:** Original requester
    
    **Why:** Tell requester the request was denied (+reason)
    
    **Priority:** MEDIUM
    
    **Actions:** —
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Listing Approval Workflow

**Category:** `properties` · **Subcategory:** `listing`

<Note>
Setting visibility requires `real_estate.view`, `real_estate.manage`, or `real_estate.listing.publish` permission.
</Note>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Listing Approval Requested">
    **Type:** `listing_approval_requested`
    
    **When sent:** A non-publisher submits a listing for approval
    
    **Sender:** Submitter
    
    **Recipients:** Org `real_estate.manage` approvers
    
    **Why:** Approve or reject the submission
    
    **Priority:** HIGH
    
    **Actions:** Review listing
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Listing Approved">
    **Type:** `listing_approved`
    
    **When sent:** A manager approves a submitted listing
    
    **Sender:** Approver
    
    **Recipients:** Listing publisher (`createdBy`)
    
    **Why:** Listing approved (may be live)
    
    **Priority:** HIGH
    
    **Actions:** View listing
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Listing Rejected">
    **Type:** `listing_rejected`
    
    **When sent:** A manager rejects a submitted listing
    
    **Sender:** Rejecter
    
    **Recipients:** Listing publisher (`createdBy`)
    
    **Why:** Fix and resubmit (+reason)
    
    **Priority:** HIGH
    
    **Actions:** View listing
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Listing Deleted">
    **Type:** `listing_deleted`
    
    **When sent:** A listing is deleted by someone other than its publisher
    
    **Sender:** Deleter
    
    **Recipients:** Listing publisher (`createdBy`)
    
    **Why:** Their listing was removed
    
    **Priority:** MEDIUM
    
    **Actions:** —
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## CRM Notifications

### Lead Assignment & Stakeholder

**Category:** `crm` · **Subcategory:** `leads`

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Lead Assignment Notifications">
    **Lead Assigned**
    - **Type:** `lead_assigned`
    - **When sent:** A lead is assigned to a user/team
    - **Sender:** Assigner
    - **Recipients:** New stakeholders (excl. assigner)
    - **Priority:** MEDIUM
    - **Actions:** View Lead

    **Lead Unassigned**
    - **Type:** `lead_unassigned`
    - **When sent:** A lead is returned to the pool
    - **Sender:** Performer
    - **Recipients:** Former stakeholders (excl. performer)
    - **Priority:** MEDIUM
    - **Actions:** —

    **Lead Claimed**
    - **Type:** `lead_claimed`
    - **When sent:** A user claims a lead from the pool
    - **Sender:** Claiming user
    - **Recipients:** Other stakeholders
    - **Priority:** MEDIUM
    - **Actions:** View Lead
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Stakeholder Management">
    **Stakeholder Added**
    - **Type:** `lead_stakeholder_added`
    - **When sent:** A user/team added as lead stakeholder (non system-initiated)
    - **Sender:** Adder
    - **Recipients:** Added user
    - **Priority:** MEDIUM
    - **Actions:** View Lead

    **Stakeholder Removed**
    - **Type:** `lead_stakeholder_removed`
    - **When sent:** A stakeholder removed from a lead
    - **Sender:** Remover
    - **Recipients:** Removed user (not self)
    - **Priority:** MEDIUM
    - **Actions:** —

    **Commission Changed**
    - **Type:** `lead_stakeholder_commission_changed`
    - **When sent:** A lead stakeholder's commission % changes
    - **Sender:** Changer
    - **Recipients:** Affected user
    - **Priority:** LOW
    - **Actions:** View Lead
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Primary Assignee Management">
    **Primary Assignee Assigned**
    - **Type:** `lead_primary_assignee_assigned`
    - **When sent:** A user or team-pool row is made primary on a lead
    - **Sender:** Changer
    - **Recipients:** Affected user, or each team leader when the new primary is a team pool (not self)
    - **Priority:** MEDIUM
    - **Actions:** View Lead

    **Primary Assignee Unassigned**
    - **Type:** `lead_primary_assignee_unassigned`
    - **When sent:** A user or team-pool row loses primary on a lead
    - **Sender:** Changer
    - **Recipients:** Affected user, or each team leader when the former primary was a team pool (not self)
    - **Priority:** MEDIUM
    - **Actions:** View Lead

    **Primary Assignee Row Changed**
    - **Type:** `lead_primary_assignee_row_changed`
    - **When sent:** Primary assignment moves between stakeholder rows (same user)
    - **Sender:** Changer
    - **Recipients:** Affected user
    - **Priority:** MEDIUM
    - **Actions:** View Lead
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
System-initiated stakeholder creations (distribution/escalation engines) emit `lead_assigned` instead of `lead_stakeholder_added` to avoid duplicate/confusing pings.
</Tip>

### Activity Stakeholder Inbox

**Category:** `crm` · **Subcategory:** `activities`

<Info>
Triggered when an activity is logged/scheduled on a lead or contact. Sender = the performer; recipients = lead+linked-contact stakeholders with `team.crm.view`/`team.crm.manage` (excl. performer). All MEDIUM priority, IN_APP + EMAIL candidates.
</Info>

**Activity Types:**
- `activity_call_logged`
- `activity_email_logged`
- `activity_sms_logged`
- `activity_meeting_scheduled`
- `activity_site_visit_scheduled`
- `activity_note_added`
- `activity_marked_important`

**Actions (all types):** View record (navigates using the payload `entityType` / `entityId`)

<Warning>
Create/schedule only — editing an activity does NOT trigger notifications.
</Warning>
