## The 3-Month Free Trial Strategy

The centerpiece of our go-to-market approach allows potential customers to experience the full value of Pro or Business plans risk-free.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Card Capture">
    Customer adds payment card at checkout but is charged AED 0 for 90 days
  </Step>
  <Step title="Full Access">
    Trial users get complete access to their chosen plan features
  </Step>
  <Step title="Conversion or Cancellation">
    Cancel before day 90 with no charge, or auto-convert to paid plan
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
The trial waives only the subscription fee. AI credits and overages are still charged to prevent abuse of cost-of-goods during the trial period.
</Warning>

## Record Counting Rules

<Info>
Import vs. In-App Creation: Only records created within the app count toward tier limits. Imported records are always unlimited on every tier.
</Info>

| Record Source | Counts Toward Limit? |
|---------------|---------------------|
| CSV/Excel/Bulk Import | **No** - Always unlimited |
| Manual creation in app | Yes |
| Auto-created from channels | Yes |
| Created from listings/properties | Yes |
| API/automation created | Yes |

This approach removes migration barriers while still encouraging upgrades based on organic growth and feature usage.

## Unified AI Credit System

All AI features draw from a single organizational credit pool, creating a simplified billing model and encouraging platform engagement.

### Credit Costs

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Propilot">
    - Lite model: 20 credits
    - Standard model: 60 credits  
    - Max model: 100 credits
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="AI Messaging">
    - Auto-reply generation: 40 credits
    - Only AI-generated replies consume credits
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Valuations">
    - Unit valuation: 300 credits
    - Charged every time (cache or fresh)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Credit Economics

- **Value anchor:** 20 credits = AED 1 (1 credit ≈ AED 0.05)
- **Gross margin:** 75-85% over model cost-of-goods
- **Cache strategy:** Cached valuations still cost 300 credits (~100% margin)

### Out of Credits Handling

<Steps>
  <Step title="Early Warning">
    Alert owners/admins at 80% consumption via app and email
  </Step>
  <Step title="Depletion Alert">
    Final warning at 100% consumption
  </Step>
  <Step title="Graceful Degradation">
    AI auto-replies mark conversations as "needs human" instead of going silent
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hard Stop">
    All AI features display upgrade/top-up prompts
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Feature Gating Strategy

### Always Public (Including Free)
- Core CRM functionality
- Off-plan and secondary directories  
- Developer portal
- Teams & hierarchy
- Advanced analytics
- Lead/deal transfer system

### Paid Tier Features
- **Distribution & escalation engines** (Pro+)
- **Commission tracking** (Pro+)
- **Custom roles** (Pro+)
- **Extended audit logs** (Business)

### Metered Features
- **Unit valuations** - 300 credits per valuation
- **AI messaging** - 40 credits per auto-reply
- **Propilot queries** - 20-100 credits based on model
