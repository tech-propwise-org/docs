## The 3-month free trial (growth wedge)

The centerpiece of go-to-market: anyone can subscribe to **Pro or Business** by adding a card, get the **full plan experience for 90 days at AED 0**, and cancel anytime before day 90 with no charge.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Card capture at checkout">
    Card captured at checkout; **AED 0 charged** until day 90.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Full access during trial">
    During trial: subscription status is `TRIALING` and grants **full access to the chosen plan**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Cancel or convert">
    - Cancel before day 90 → no charge → downgrade to Free
    - No cancel → auto-converts to the paid plan on day 90
    - Reminder sent ~3 days before trial ends
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
**Business trial** lets you onboard a **large agency** risk-free. 50+-seat agencies still come through contact-sales, where the trial terms are confirmed as part of the deal.
</Info>

### Margin protection

<Warning>
The trial waives only the **subscription fee**. The unified AI-credit wallet still applies at the plan's levels during the trial, and overage still requires buying top-up packs (a real charge). This prevents 90 days of free AI cost-of-goods — which matters most on Business, where the included credit pool is large.
</Warning>

<Check>
Most of the plumbing already exists: Stripe checkout accepts `trialPeriodDays`, and the subscription entity has `trialEndsAt` + a `TRIALING` status. Remaining work is enforcement wiring + the `trial_will_end` reminder webhook.
</Check>

### How entity caps are counted (imports vs in-app creation)

The lead / contact / deal / company caps apply **only to records created inside the app**. **Imported records never count** against the cap and are always unlimited on every tier.

| Source of record | Counts toward cap? |
| --- | --- |
| CSV / Excel / bulk import | **No** — always unlimited |
| Manual create (Add Lead / Add Contact / …) | Yes |
| Auto-created from a channel (WhatsApp / Meta inbound) | Yes |
| Created from a listing / property interest | Yes |
| Created by distribution / automation / API | Yes |

<Tip>
**Why:** Brokerages can migrate their entire existing database in for free (removes the #1 onboarding blocker), while the cap still meters ongoing *organic* growth created through the product.
</Tip>

<Warning>
**Trade-off to be aware of:** Because imports are uncapped, record counts are a soft lever, not a hard paywall — a team could bulk-import everything and rarely hit the cap. That's intentional here: the real upgrade drivers become **features** (distribution, analytics, teams), **seats**, and the **unified AI-credit wallet** (Propilot, AI messaging, valuations), not raw record volume.
</Warning>

<Info>
**Implementation note:** Enforcing this requires the counter to be **source-aware** — increment only on in-app creation paths, and skip the import path entirely.
</Info>

## Credit system (one unified wallet)

All metered AI usage draws from **one balance** — there are no separate AI / messaging / valuation buckets. The org gets a monthly pool of **AI credits**, pooled across all seats, and spends it on whatever it wants (chat, auto-reply, or valuations).

### Per-action credit cost

<Info>
Credits are denominated at **20 credits = AED 1 of value** (the ×20 factor keeps in-app numbers large and decoupled from cash, so customers reason in credits, not dirhams).
</Info>

| Action | Credits | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| AI message reply (auto-reply) | 20 | only **AI-generated** replies burn credits; priced at the Lite floor so high-volume messaging is affordable for agencies |
| Propilot prompt — lite model | 20 | cheapest model tier |
| Propilot prompt — standard model | 60 | default tier |
| Propilot prompt — max model | 100 | flagship model (gpt-5.5) |
| Deep web research surcharge | +30 | added **on top** of the per-tier prompt cost when the Market & Regulations persona runs a `deep` web-research turn that actually scrapes the web (Fast research adds nothing) |
| Knowledge-base ingestion | 20 + 5/extra chunk | **dynamic** — base 20 (≤1 chunk) + 5 per additional chunk, capped at 1,000, so a large document/site costs proportionally more than a one-line FAQ |
| **Unit valuation** | 300 | charged **every time** (cache or fresh) |

### Monthly allowance (pooled at org level)

| Tier | Allowance | Min-seat org total |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Free | 2,000 / org | 2,000 |
| Pro | 5,000 / seat | 25,000 (5 seats) |
| Business | 9,000 / seat | 90,000 (10 seats) |

<Tip>
**Pooled** means a heavy agent draws from the shared org balance — there is no hard per-seat cap. Top-up packs refill the same wallet, sold as **non-round bundles** with the per-credit cash rate never advertised.
</Tip>

### Economics

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Value anchor & margins">
    - Internal value anchor: **20 credits = AED 1** (1 credit ≈ AED 0.05)
    - Retail credits sell at ~**75–85% gross margin** over model cost-of-goods — well above the 30–50% floor — because we price the **outcome**, not the tokens
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Cache economics">
    **Always charge on cache:** a valuation costs 300 credits even when served from the 7-day cache (cache hits cost ~AED 0 → ~100% margin on those).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="External costs">
    **Meta fees are external:** WhatsApp/Meta conversation & template charges are paid by the customer directly to Meta (their own WABA). They are **not** in the wallet — only the AI model cost of an auto-reply is.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Out of credits

When the wallet hits zero, the AI **hard-stops with a warning** ("Propilot is out of credits — buy a top-up or upgrade").

<Steps>
  <Step title="Proactive alerts">
    Owner/admins are alerted at **80%** and **100%** consumption (in-app + email).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Graceful degradation for auto-reply">
    On the **AI auto-reply** path, the conversation is set to **needs human** (and/or an AI draft is saved) instead of going silent, so an agent can step in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Hard stop for other features">
    Propilot and unit valuation show a clear "out of credits" message with upgrade/top-up CTA.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Pricing rationale

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Pro tier positioning (AED 199/agent)">
    **AED 199/agent (~$54)** positions Pro as a premium, full-stack RE CRM (AI + WhatsApp + off-plan + distribution/escalation) rather than a budget tool — and comfortably absorbs AI cost-of-goods.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Seat floor economics">
    **Seat floor (Pro min 5 / Business min 10)** sets a meaningful revenue floor per account:
    - Pro ≥ AED 995/mo (~$270)
    - Business ≥ AED 2,690/mo (~$732)
    
    This targets real brokerages rather than solo agents or micro teams.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Business tier premium (AED 299/agent)">
    The **AED 100 premium** (~50% uplift) over Pro delivers:
    - Unlimited entity caps (no record limits)
    - 80% more credits per seat (9,000 vs 5,000)
    - Unlimited audit log retention
    - Priority support
    
    Designed for agencies that run high-volume operations and need enterprise-grade capabilities.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Annual discount strategy">
    **~20% annual discount** (Pro: 199 → 159, Business: 299 → 239) incentivizes cash-flow commitment while maintaining healthy margins. Annual contracts improve retention and reduce churn friction.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Implementation notes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Stripe checkout configuration">
    Configure Stripe checkout to accept `trialPeriodDays: 90` for Pro and Business plans. Ensure card capture happens upfront with AED 0 initial charge.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Subscription status handling">
    Wire up `TRIALING` status to grant full plan access. Implement `trial_will_end` webhook for 3-day reminder emails.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Entity counter logic">
    Implement source-aware counters that:
    - Skip incrementing for CSV/Excel/bulk imports
    - Increment for manual creates, channel inbounds, and API creates
    - Store the source flag on each record for audit purposes
  </Step>

  <Step title="Credit wallet enforcement">
    Build the unified credit wallet with:
    - Pooled org-level balance
    - Per-action deduction logic
    - 80% and 100% threshold alerts
    - Graceful degradation (needs human flag) for auto-reply
    - Hard stop + upgrade CTA for Propilot and valuations
  </Step>

  <Step title="Storage add-on billing">
    Create stackable storage SKUs as recurring Stripe subscription line items. Each +25 GB block is a separate quantity-based line item at AED 99/mo.
  </Step>
</Steps>

All pricing, feature gates, and credit costs should be configured in a central pricing configuration file (not hardcoded) to support A/B testing and market adjustments post-launch.
