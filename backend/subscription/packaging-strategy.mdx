## The 3-Month Free Trial (Growth Wedge)

<Note>
The centerpiece of go-to-market: anyone can subscribe to **Pro or Business** by adding a card, get the **full plan experience for 90 days at AED 0**, and cancel anytime before day 90 with no charge.
</Note>

### How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Card Capture">
    Card captured at checkout with **AED 0 charged** until day 90
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Trial Period">
    During trial: subscription status is `TRIALING` and grants **full access to the chosen plan**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Cancel or Convert">
    - Cancel before day 90 → no charge → downgrade to Free
    - No cancel → auto-converts to the paid plan on day 90
    - Reminder sent ~3 days before trial ends
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Business trial** lets you onboard a **large agency** risk-free. 50+-seat agencies still come through contact-sales, where the trial terms are confirmed as part of the deal.

### Margin Protection

<Warning>
The trial waives only the **subscription fee**. The unified AI-credit wallet still applies at the plan's levels during the trial, and overage still requires buying top-up packs (a real charge). This prevents 90 days of free AI cost-of-goods — which matters most on Business, where the included credit pool is large.
</Warning>

<Check>
Most of the plumbing already exists: Stripe checkout accepts `trialPeriodDays`, and the subscription entity has `trialEndsAt` + a `TRIALING` status. Remaining work is enforcement wiring + the `trial_will_end` reminder webhook.
</Check>

### How Entity Caps Are Counted

<Info>
The lead / contact / deal / company caps apply **only to records created inside the app**. **Imported records never count** against the cap and are always unlimited on every tier.
</Info>

| Source of Record | Counts Toward Cap? |
| --- | --- |
| CSV / Excel / bulk import | **No** — always unlimited |
| Manual create (Add Lead / Add Contact / …) | Yes |
| Auto-created from a channel (WhatsApp / Meta inbound) | Yes |
| Created from a listing / property interest | Yes |
| Created by distribution / automation / API | Yes |

#### Why This Approach?

Brokerages can migrate their entire existing database in for free (removes the #1 onboarding blocker), while the cap still meters ongoing *organic* growth created through the product.

<Warning>
**Trade-off to be aware of:** Because imports are uncapped, record counts are a soft lever, not a hard paywall — a team could bulk-import everything and rarely hit the cap. That's intentional here: the real upgrade drivers become **features** (distribution, analytics, teams), **seats**, and the **unified AI-credit wallet** (Propilot, AI messaging, valuations), not raw record volume.
</Warning>

<Tip>
**Implementation note:** Enforcing this requires the counter to be **source-aware** — increment only on in-app creation paths, and skip the import path entirely.
</Tip>

## Credit System (One Unified Wallet)

All metered AI usage draws from **one balance** — there are no separate AI / messaging / valuation buckets. The org gets a monthly pool of **AI credits**, pooled across all seats, and spends it on whatever it wants (chat, auto-reply, or valuations).

<Info>
Credits are denominated at **20 credits = AED 1 of value** (the ×20 factor keeps in-app numbers large and decoupled from cash, so customers reason in credits, not dirhams).
</Info>

### Per-Action Credit Cost

<Note>
Prices were recalibrated 2026-07-15 against measured per-action COGS (gpt-5.6 tier models) to a **uniform ~10% COGS-to-face-value ratio** (`credits ≈ 200 × COGS_AED`), which makes the full-allowance burn cost mix-invariant (≈ AED 20–25 on a 5,000-credit Pro seat regardless of action mix).
</Note>

| Action | Credits | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| AI message reply (auto-reply) | 5 | Only **AI-generated** replies burn credits; tiny-context action (~6K tokens) — 5 credits ≈ 1,000 replies/month on a Pro seat |
| Propilot prompt — lite model | 20 | Cheapest model tier |
| Propilot prompt — standard model | 45 | Default tier (gpt-5.6-luna) |
| Propilot prompt — max model | 100 | Flagship model (gpt-5.6-terra) |
| AI agent prompt optimize | 5 | Near-zero COGS; floor-priced so users are not discouraged from improving agent instructions |
| Deep web research surcharge | +30 | Added **on top** of the per-tier prompt cost when the Market & Regulations persona runs a `deep` web-research turn that actually scrapes the web (Fast research adds nothing) |
| Knowledge-base ingestion | 20 + 5/extra chunk | **Dynamic** — base 20 (≤1 chunk) + 5 per additional chunk, capped at 1,000, so a large document/site costs proportionally more than a one-line FAQ |
| **Unit valuation** | 300 | Charged **every time** (cache or fresh); value-priced premium anchor |

### Monthly Allowance (Pooled at Org Level)

| Tier | Allowance | Min-Seat Org Total |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Free | 2,000 / org | 2,000 |
| Pro | 5,000 / seat | 25,000 (5 seats) |
| Business | 9,000 / seat | 90,000 (10 seats) |

<Tip>
**Pooled** means a heavy agent draws from the shared org balance — there is no hard per-seat cap. Top-up packs refill the same wallet, sold as **non-round bundles** with the per-credit cash rate never advertised.
</Tip>

### Economics

### Out of Credits

<Warning>
When the wallet hits zero, the AI **hard-stops with a warning** ("Propilot is out of credits — buy a top-up or upgrade").
</Warning>

To avoid dropping live leads:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Alert at 80%">
    Owner/admins are alerted at **80%** consumption (in-app + email)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Alert at 100%">
    Final alert when credits are fully depleted
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Graceful Degradation">
    On the **AI auto-reply** path, the conversation is set to **needs human** (and/or an AI draft is saved) instead of going silent, so an agent can step in
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Summary

<Check>
This packaging strategy balances **aggressive customer acquisition** (3-month free trial) with **margin protection** (metered AI credits even during trial) and **clear upgrade paths** (features + seats + credits as levers).
</Check>

The **unified credit wallet** simplifies customer reasoning ("one balance for everything AI") while preserving pricing flexibility (adjust per-action costs independently). **Import-unlimited, creation-capped** entity counting removes the #1 onboarding friction (migration) while still driving upgrades through organic growth.

**Open items** require final business approval, particularly around volume discount structure and trial scope, but the core architecture is ready for implementation.
