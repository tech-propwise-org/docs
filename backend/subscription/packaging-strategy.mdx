## Overview

This document outlines the comprehensive subscription packaging strategy for PropWise CRM, designed specifically for the UAE real estate market. The strategy centers around a simplified three-tier model with an aggressive growth wedge through 3-month free trials.

**Status:** Proposed (pending final sign-off on open items in Section 7)

## The 3-month free trial strategy

The centerpiece of our go-to-market approach allows prospects to experience the full value of Pro or Business plans risk-free for 90 days.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Trial initiation">
    Customer adds payment card at checkout but is charged AED 0 for the first 90 days
  </Step>
  <Step title="Full access period">
    Subscription status shows as `TRIALING` with complete access to chosen plan features
  </Step>
  <Step title="Conversion or cancellation">
    Customer can cancel before day 90 (no charge) or auto-convert to paid plan
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reminder system">
    Automated reminder sent ~3 days before trial ends
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
The trial waives only the subscription fee. The unified AI-credit wallet still applies at plan levels during trial, and overage charges for credit top-ups remain active to protect margins.
</Warning>

All pricing is in AED, per agent, per month. Annual billing offers approximately 20% discount.

    - 8,000 AI credits/seat/month
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Business Tier">
    **AED 299/month** (annual ~239)
    - 10+ seats with volume discount

## Unified AI credit system

All AI-powered features draw from a single credit wallet, providing flexibility while maintaining cost control.

### Credit pricing structure
Credits are denominated at **20 credits = AED 1** to keep in-app numbers intuitive.

| Action | Credit Cost | Notes |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| Propilot prompt (lite) | 20 | Cheapest model tier |
| AI message auto-reply | 40 | Only AI-generated replies |
| Propilot prompt (standard) | 60 | Default tier |
| Propilot prompt (max) | 100 | Flagship model |
| **Unit valuation** | 300 | Charged every time (cache or fresh) |

### Monthly allowances (pooled org-wide)

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Free">
    **2,000 credits/org**
    - Shared across all users
    - ~100 lite prompts or ~6 unit valuations
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Pro">
    **8,000 credits/seat**
    - Minimum 40,000 credits (5 seats)
    - Pooled across organization
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Business">
    **12,000 credits/seat**
    - Minimum 120,000 credits (10 seats)
    - Higher allowance for power users
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Credit depletion handling

<Steps>
  <Step title="Early warning">
    Owner/admin alerts at 80% consumption via in-app notification and email
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hard stop">
    At 100% consumption, AI features show "out of credits" warning
  </Step>
  <Step title="Graceful degradation">
    AI auto-reply sets conversations to "needs human" instead of going silent
  </Step>
  <Step title="Top-up options">
    Non-round credit bundles available for immediate purchase
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add-on packages

### Extra storage
- **+25 GB blocks** at **AED 99/month**
- Stackable and recurring
- Same price for Pro & Business (not available on Free)
- ~90-95% gross margin

### AI credit top-ups
- Non-round bundles to obscure per-credit pricing
- Refills the unified wallet immediately
- 75-85% gross margin over model costs

## Pricing rationale

The AED 199/agent positioning for Pro establishes PropWise as a premium, full-stack real estate CRM rather than a budget tool. Key strategic benefits:

- **Revenue floors**: Pro minimum AED 995/month (5 seats), Business minimum AED 2,690/month (10 seats)
- **Market positioning**: Premium pricing that comfortably absorbs AI cost-of-goods  
- **Risk mitigation**: 3-month trial removes commitment barriers for higher price points
- **Scale focus**: Seat minimums target real brokerages over individual agents

<Check>
Business tier now differentiates primarily on scale (unlimited limits, larger credit allowances, priority support) rather than exclusive features, making the upgrade path clearer for growing organizations.
</Check>
