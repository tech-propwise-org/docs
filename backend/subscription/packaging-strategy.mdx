## Summary of decisions

The PropWise CRM subscription strategy centers around a **3-month free trial growth wedge** with three tiers: Free, Pro, and Business. All pricing is in AED with flat per-agent pricing and a unified AI-credit system.

### Key strategic decisions

| Decision | Choice |
| --- | --- |
| Currency | **AED** native (charged in AED on Stripe) |
| Tiers | **Free → Pro → Business** (Starter dropped) |
| Growth wedge | **3-month free trial on Pro & Business**, card upfront, charged only after day 90 |
| Seat model | **Pro 5–10 seats** (11+ must upgrade to Business); **Business 10+ seats with volume discount** |
| Off-plan directory | **Public to everyone** (incl. Free) |
| Universal CRM features | **Teams & hierarchy, advanced analytics, and lead/deal transfer** available on all tiers |
| Real-estate monetization | **Unit valuation** (metered via credits) |
| Communication caps | **Unlimited on Pro & Business** (Free = 1 each) |
| Entity caps | **Imported records unlimited**; only **in-app created** records count toward caps |
| Metered usage | **One unified AI-credit wallet** for Propilot, AI messaging, and valuations |

## The 3-month free trial (growth wedge)

<Tip>
The centerpiece of go-to-market: anyone can subscribe to **Pro or Business** by adding a card, get the **full plan experience for 90 days at AED 0**, and cancel anytime before day 90 with no charge.
</Tip>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Card capture at checkout">
    Customer provides payment details but **AED 0 charged** until day 90
  </Step>
  <Step title="Full trial access">
    During trial: subscription status is `TRIALING` and grants **full access to the chosen plan**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Trial completion">
    - Cancel before day 90 → no charge → downgrade to Free
    - No cancel → auto-converts to paid plan on day 90
    - Reminder sent ~3 days before trial ends
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Margin protection during trial

<Warning>
The trial waives only the **subscription fee**. The unified AI-credit wallet still applies at the plan's levels during the trial, and overage still requires buying top-up packs (a real charge). This prevents 90 days of free AI cost-of-goods.
</Warning>

**Business trial benefits:** Lets you onboard a **large agency** risk-free. 50+-seat agencies still come through contact-sales, where trial terms are confirmed as part of the deal.

### Universal features (available on all tiers)

<Check>
These core features are available to **all users, including Free tier**:
- Core CRM: leads, contacts, deals, companies, pipeline, tasks
- **Off-plan directory** - public access for everyone
- **Secondary listings directory** - public access for everyone  
- **Developer portal** - separate authentication system
- **Teams & hierarchy** - team management with team-scoped roles
- **Advanced analytics** - `/analytics` reporting module with performance metrics
- **Lead / deal transfer system** - assignment handoff with approval workflow
</Check>

### Entity cap calculation (imports vs in-app creation)

<Info>
The lead / contact / deal / company caps apply **only to records created inside the app**. **Imported records never count** against the cap and are always unlimited on every tier.
</Info>

| Source of record | Counts toward cap? |
| --- | --- |
| CSV / Excel / bulk import | **No** — always unlimited |
| Manual create (Add Lead / Add Contact / …) | Yes |
| Auto-created from a channel (WhatsApp / Meta inbound) | Yes |
| Created from a listing / property interest | Yes |
| Created by distribution / automation / API | Yes |

**Why this matters:** Brokerages can migrate their entire existing database for free (removes the #1 onboarding blocker), while the cap still meters ongoing *organic* growth created through the product.

## Credit system (unified wallet)

All metered AI usage draws from **one balance** across the organization. There are no separate AI / messaging / valuation buckets.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Credit Value" icon="coins">
    **20 credits = AED 1** of value (keeps in-app numbers large and decoupled from cash)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pooled Balance" icon="users">
    Monthly allowance is **pooled at org level** - heavy users draw from shared balance
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Per-action credit costs

| Action | Credits | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Propilot prompt — lite model | 20 | cheapest model tier |
| AI message reply (auto-reply) | 40 | only **AI-generated** replies burn credits |
| Propilot prompt — standard model | 60 | default tier |
| Propilot prompt — max model | 100 | flagship model (gpt-5.5) |
| **Unit valuation** | 300 | charged **every time** (cache or fresh) |

### Monthly allowances

| Tier | Allowance | Min-seat org total |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Free | 2,000 / org | 2,000 |
| Pro | 8,000 / seat | 40,000 (5 seats) |
| Business | 12,000 / seat | 120,000 (10 seats) |

### Out of credits behavior

<Steps>
  <Step title="Early warnings">
    Owner/admins are alerted at **80%** and **100%** consumption (in-app + email)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hard stop">
    When wallet hits zero, AI **hard-stops with warning** ("Propilot is out of credits — buy a top-up or upgrade")
  </Step>
  <Step title="Auto-reply fallback">
    On AI auto-reply path, conversation is set to **needs human** instead of going silent, so an agent can step in
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Economics

- Internal value anchor: **20 credits = AED 1** (1 credit ≈ AED 0.05)
- Retail credits sell at ~**75–85% gross margin** over model cost-of-goods
- **Always charge on cache:** valuation costs 300 credits even when served from 7-day cache
- **Meta fees are external:** WhatsApp/Meta charges paid by customer directly to Meta

## Add-on packs (Pro & Business)

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="AI Credit Top-up Packs">
    - Refill the unified wallet
    - Sold as non-round bundles
    - Per-credit cash rate not advertised to customers
    - Maintains pricing opacity and value perception
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Extra Storage">
    - **+25 GB blocks at AED 99 / month**
    - Recurring and **stackable**
    - **Same price on Pro & Business** (not offered on Free)
    - COGS ~AED 5–9/block → ~90–95% margin
    - Billed as recurring per-unit Stripe subscription line item
  </Accordion>

## Pricing rationale

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Pro Tier Strategy">
    **AED 199/agent (~$54)** positions Pro as a premium, full-stack RE CRM rather than a budget tool:
    - Includes AI + WhatsApp + off-plan + distribution/escalation
    - Comfortably absorbs AI cost-of-goods
    - **Seat floor (min 5)** sets revenue floor of AED 995/mo per account
    - Targets real brokerages rather than single agents
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Business Tier Value">
    **AED 299/agent (~$81)** differentiates on scale and premium support:
    - **Min 10 seats** sets revenue floor of AED 2,690/mo per account
    - Larger credit allowance (12,000 vs 8,000/seat)
    - Unlimited audit retention and priority support
    - Volume discounts for large deployments
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Trial Impact">
    **3-month free trial** on both Pro and Business:
    - Offsets higher entry price by removing commitment risk
    - Enables landing large agencies risk-free
    - Card upfront ensures qualified prospects
    - Auto-conversion after 90 days drives retention
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

The strategy balances premium positioning with growth acceleration, using the trial period to overcome price resistance while maintaining healthy unit economics through the unified credit system and add-on revenue streams.
