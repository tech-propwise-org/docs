## Overview

Our subscription packaging strategy is designed specifically for the UAE real estate market, offering a three-tier structure with an aggressive growth model centered around risk-free trials and unified AI credits.

<Info>
All pricing is in **AED (Arab Emirates Dirham)** and charged natively through Stripe for the Dubai real estate market.
</Info>

## Core Strategy Decisions

The following strategic decisions form the foundation of our packaging approach:

| Decision | Choice |
|----------|--------|
| Currency | **AED** native (charged in AED on Stripe) |
| Tiers | **Free → Pro → Business** (Starter tier dropped) |
| Growth wedge | **3-month free trial** on Pro & Business with card upfront |
| Seat model | **Flat per-agent pricing** with tier-specific minimums |
| Real estate monetization | **Unit valuation** metered via unified AI credits |
| Entity caps | **Imported records unlimited**; only in-app created records count |

## The 3-Month Free Trial

<CardGroup cols={2}>
<Card title="Risk-Free Onboarding" icon="credit-card">
Capture card details but charge AED 0 for 90 days, allowing full plan access
</Card>
<Card title="Margin Protection" icon="shield">
Trial waives subscription fees but AI credits still apply with real overage charges
</Card>
</CardGroup>

### How It Works

<Steps>
<Step title="Initial Signup">
Customer subscribes to Pro or Business with card details captured at checkout
</Step>
<Step title="Trial Period">
Full plan access for 90 days with subscription status `TRIALING` and AED 0 charges
</Step>
<Step title="Trial End Options">
- Cancel before day 90: No charge, downgrade to Free
- No action: Auto-convert to paid plan on day 90
</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Customers receive reminder notifications approximately 3 days before trial expiration.
</Note>

## Pricing Tiers

### Free Tier
- **Price**: AED 0
- **Users**: Up to 2
- **Target**: Individual agents or very small teams

### Pro Tier  
- **Price**: AED 199/agent/month (AED 159 annual)
- **Users**: 5-10 seats (11+ must upgrade to Business)
- **Target**: Small to medium brokerages

### Business Tier
- **Price**: AED 299/agent/month (AED 239 annual) 
- **Users**: 10+ seats with volume discounts
- **Target**: Large brokerages and enterprises

<Warning>
Teams with 50+ seats require contact-sales consultation to confirm trial terms as part of the enterprise deal process.
</Warning>

## Entity Counting Rules

<Warning>
Understanding how entity caps are calculated is crucial for proper tier positioning.
</Warning>

### What Counts vs. What Doesn't

| Record Source | Counts Toward Cap? |
|---------------|-------------------|
| **CSV/Excel/Bulk Import** | **No** — Always unlimited |
| Manual creation (Add Lead/Contact) | Yes |
| Auto-created from channels (WhatsApp/Meta) | Yes |
| Created from listing/property interest | Yes |
| Created by automation/API | Yes |

<Tip>
This approach removes the biggest onboarding blocker by allowing unlimited data migration while still creating upgrade pressure through organic growth.
</Tip>

## Unified AI Credit System

All AI-powered features draw from a single, shared credit wallet at the organization level.

### Credit Economics

<Info>
Credits are valued at **20 credits = AED 1** to keep in-app numbers meaningful while decoupling from direct cash pricing.
</Info>

### Per-Action Credit Costs

| Action | Credits | AED Value |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| Propilot prompt (lite model) | 20 | ~AED 1.00 |
| AI message auto-reply | 40 | ~AED 2.00 |
| Propilot prompt (standard model) | 60 | ~AED 3.00 |
| Propilot prompt (max model) | 100 | ~AED 5.00 |
| **Unit valuation** | 300 | ~AED 15.00 |

### Monthly Credit Allowances

| Tier | Credits per Seat | Minimum Org Total |
|------|------------------|-------------------|
| Free | 2,000/org | 2,000 |
| Pro | 8,000/seat | 40,000 (5 seats) |
| Business | 12,000/seat | 120,000 (10 seats) |

<Note>
Credits are **pooled at the organization level** — there are no individual seat limits within the shared wallet.
</Note>

### Credit Depletion Handling

<Steps>
<Step title="Early Warning">
Owner/admin alerts at 80% credit consumption via in-app notifications and email
</Step>
<Step title="Final Warning">
Additional alerts at 100% consumption with upgrade/top-up prompts
</Step>
<Step title="Hard Stop">
AI features disabled with clear messaging about purchasing top-ups or upgrading
</Step>
<Step title="Lead Protection">
AI auto-reply conversations marked as "needs human" instead of going silent
</Step>
</Steps>

## Add-On Products

### AI Credit Top-Up Packs
- Refill the unified credit wallet
- Sold as non-round bundles
- Per-credit cash rate not advertised to maintain value perception
- Available on Pro and Business tiers

### Extra Storage
- **+25 GB blocks** at **AED 99/month**
- Recurring and stackable
- Same pricing on Pro and Business
- Not available on Free tier
- ~90-95% gross margin

<Check>
Storage add-ons are billed as recurring Stripe subscription line items following the main plan's billing cycle.
</Check>

## Implementation Considerations

### Technical Requirements
- Stripe integration with `trialPeriodDays` parameter
- Subscription status tracking (`TRIALING` state)  
- Credit wallet system with pooled organization-level balances
- Source-aware entity counting (imports vs. in-app creation)
- Usage alerting at 80% and 100% thresholds

### Business Metrics to Track
- Trial-to-paid conversion rates
- Average revenue per seat by tier
- Credit consumption patterns
- Storage utilization and add-on adoption
- Churn rates by tier and cohort
